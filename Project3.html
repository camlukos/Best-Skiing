Where Should I Go Skiing?

























This project will inform avid skiers on how to best plan their ski trips, based on historical snow data. Snow data from the past 5 years will be utilized to create month-to-month visualizations on where the highest snowfall occurred.


Data and visualizations are are provided as part of the analysis, including explanations and descriptions of trends and correlations.
